Project Summary

Topic and Purpose

My website is a personal portfolio. It tells my readers about who I am and what I do: my story, background, character/personality; achievements; ambitions. There are distinct pages for About (details about early and personal life), Work (activities and YouTube channel), Gallery (portraits and photography), Blog (personal reflections, Instagram stories and posts) and Contact (social media handles and a feedback form).

Extras Used

I used the extras form(which submits to a page) and iframes. The form was a feedback form for users to give recommendations on how to improve my website. The iframes were primarily used for embedded YouTube videos.
