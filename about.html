ABOUT

Hello!˙

I am GyuMin Eric Gabriel Choi (Korean: 최규민; Chinese: 崔圭玟; born June 27, 2003), a Korean international scholar, musician, YouTuber and an aspiring lawyer and policymaker.


Early life

I was born in Seoul, South Korea, on June 27, 2003. I moved to Vancouver, Canada with my family when I was 3, and then moved to Shanghai, China in 2007. Then I moved with my family back to Seoul in 2011. When I was 9, my family moved to Beijing, China in 2012 and attended Dulwich College Beijing, a British international school in Beijing. During my time there, I served as the Student Council Chairperson and founded the Current Affairs Society. I spent a portion of my summer in 2019 taking a course on Politics at Stanford University. I spent the summer of 2020 working as an intern at the Pangoal Institution, a China-based public policy thinktank. In 2021, I graduated from Dulwich College Beijing.˙

Now, I am a freshman at the University of Southern California, Los Angeles, California.


Influencer

I am a video content creator on YouTube. I make music cover videos, and post vlogs about current affairs and my life.


Personal life.

I speak fluent English, Korean and Chinese. My interests include singing, reading, writing.




I feel incredibly privileged to share what I am learning and connect with so many incredible people and here on CHOI.com. Thank you for reading!
